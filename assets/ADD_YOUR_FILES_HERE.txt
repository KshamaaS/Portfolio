FILES TO ADD IN THIS FOLDER:

1. profile.jpg
   - Your professional photo
   - Recommended: Square image, at least 400x400 pixels
   - Will appear on the home page

2. resume.pdf
   - Your resume/CV
   - Will be linked for download

3. projects/ folder
   - Add screenshots of your projects here
   - Reference them in projects.json like: "image": "assets/projects/myproject.png"
   - Recommended size: 800x500 pixels or similar aspect ratio

4. publications/ folder
   - Add book covers here
   - Reference them in publications.json like: "image": "assets/publications/book-cover.png"
   - Recommended size: 300x400 pixels or similar book ratio

Tips for photos:
- Use a professional, well-lit photo
- Simple background works best
- Smile! You want to look approachable
- Crop to square for best results
